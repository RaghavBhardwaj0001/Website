About Global Trade Exchange

Building the world's most trusted network for verified international trade since 2018.

Our Story

Empowering Global Commerce

Global Trade Exchange was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize international trade by making it accessible, transparent, and profitable for businesses of all sizes.

Today, we connect over 14,700 verified buyers and sellers across 150+ countries, facilitating millions of dollars in trade deals every month. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with deep market expertise to deliver verified trade leads, intelligent deal matching, and transparent commission systems.

Whether you're a first-time exporter or a multinational corporation, Global Trade Exchange provides the tools, data, and support you need to succeed in the global marketplace.

Our Services Contact Us

Our Mission

To create the most transparent and effective B2B marketplace for international trade, empowering every business to reach global markets with verified leads and fair commission-based deals.

Our Vision

To become the world's #1 platform for cross-border trade by 2030, serving over 1 million verified businesses and facilitating $10 billion in annual trade volume across every continent.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

From a small idea to a global trade network — here's how we've grown.

2018

Founded in Mumbai
